Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Skill Tree ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Device Identifier

We generate a random, anonymous device identifier (UUID) when you first use the app. This identifier:

  • Is randomly generated and not linked to your personal identity
  • Is used solely for rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Is stored locally on your device and on our servers for rate limiting purposes

Career Goals and Skills

When you use the app, you may enter:

  • Career goals or skills you want to explore
  • Descriptions of your current skills and experience
  • Selections indicating which skills you already have

This information is sent to our servers to generate personalized career guidance using AI.

Usage Data

We collect basic usage logs including:

  • Which features you use (e.g., skill tree generation, gap analysis)
  • Timestamps of requests
  • Error information for debugging

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-generated career guidance and skill recommendations
  • Enforce rate limits to ensure fair usage for all users
  • Detect and prevent abuse of our services
  • Improve the app and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

Your career goals and skill descriptions are processed by:

  • Google Gemini API - to generate AI-powered career guidance. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare - to host our backend services and provide security. Cloudflare's privacy policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacy/

Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Your learning plans and search history are stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • Server Logs: Rate limiting data and request logs are retained for up to 7 days, then automatically deleted.
  • AI Processing: Data sent to Google Gemini is processed according to Google's data retention policies.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Cloudflare Workers)
  • No collection of personally identifiable information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete local data: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Request data deletion: Contact us to request deletion of any server-side data associated with your device ID
  • Reset your device ID: Reinstalling the app generates a new device identifier

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Disclaimer

Skill Tree provides AI-generated career guidance for informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional career counseling, educational advising, or employment services. We make no guarantees about the accuracy or completeness of the information provided.
